What Is Contact Form Marketing?

Contact form marketing is a structured outreach strategy where businesses send targeted messages to prospects via the contact forms on their websites — rather than through email or cold calls.

Unlike cold email, contact form messages land directly in a company's internal inbox — the same queue their own customers use. Decision-makers read them. They don't go to spam.

Contact Form Blaster automates this process at scale — letting you run contact form marketing campaigns across thousands of websites simultaneously, with AI-personalized messaging and real-time success tracking.

FAQ

Contact Form Marketing — Common Questions

Is contact form marketing legal?

Contact form marketing occupies a different legal space than email marketing because it does not use electronic mailing lists and is not governed by CAN-SPAM in the same way. However, users are responsible for ensuring their campaigns comply with applicable local laws and the terms of service of target websites. Contact Form Blaster is intended for legitimate business outreach only.

How is this different from email marketing?

Email marketing requires you to have a recipient's email address and relies on email servers for delivery — meaning spam filters, bounce rates, and deliverability issues are constant concerns. Contact form marketing requires only a website URL. Your message is submitted through the site's own contact form and delivered directly to their internal inbox, with none of the deliverability barriers of email.

How many websites can I target in one campaign?

Each campaign supports up to 30,000 website URLs. You can upload a CSV, XLS, or XLSX file and the platform auto-detects the URL column, removes duplicates, and begins processing. Multiple campaigns can be run simultaneously.

Do I pay if a website doesn't have a contact form?

No. You only pay for successful submissions. If a website has no contact form, or the form cannot be completed due to a system-related issue, you receive a full credit refund for that site. External failures (such as a site being offline) are charged at 50% of the credit cost.

Can I personalise each message for each website?

Yes. You can use dynamic variable tokens from your CSV columns (like {name}, {company}, {city}) to personalise messages. You can also enable AI personalization, which generates a completely unique message for each target domain based on a single instruction you provide — at a cost of 1 extra credit per site.
