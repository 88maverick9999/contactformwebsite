FAQ

Automated Form Filler — Common Questions

Does it work on all website platforms?

Yes. Because the form filler uses AI to read and understand form HTML semantically rather than relying on fixed templates, it works across virtually any website platform — WordPress, Webflow, Wix, Squarespace, Shopify, HubSpot, custom-built sites, and more. It adapts to each form's unique structure automatically.

Do I need to set up field mappings manually?

No. The AI handles all field mapping automatically. You provide your sender information once — name, email, company, phone, subject, message — and the platform figures out which form field on each website each piece of data belongs to. No manual configuration per site is needed.

What happens when a form has unusual or extra fields?

The platform supports up to 28 configurable fields — 10 required and 18 optional — including title, address, gender, budget, timeline, occupation, and more. For fields it cannot map with sufficient confidence, it skips them where optional or flags the submission accordingly. You are only charged for successful completions.

How are CAPTCHAs handled?

The form filler automatically detects reCAPTCHA v2, reCAPTCHA v3, and hCaptcha on contact forms and resolves them via an integrated third-party solving service before submitting. If a CAPTCHA is present and is successfully solved, the submission proceeds at the standard credit cost.*

Can I use my own data for each field?

Yes. You configure your campaign with your real sender details — your name, email address, company, phone number, and message. You can also use dynamic tokens like {name}, {company}, or {city} pulled from columns in your uploaded CSV, so each submission is personalised with data specific to that target.
