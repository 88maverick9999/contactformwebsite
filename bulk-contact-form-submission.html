What Is It?

What Is Bulk Contact Form Submission?

Bulk contact form submission is the process of automatically submitting a message to the contact forms of many websites simultaneously — rather than doing it manually one at a time.

Doing this manually would take days. Contact Form Blaster automates the entire process: it crawls each website, locates the contact form, maps the fields intelligently using AI, solves any CAPTCHAs it encounters, and submits your message.

The result is a scalable outreach operation that can reach tens of thousands of businesses in the time it would normally take to reach a few dozen manually.

Under the Hood

How Bulk Submission Works Technically

Every submission goes through a multi-step automated pipeline — no manual work required.

Form Discovery

The platform crawls each website URL and locates its contact form — even when it's on a subpage like /contact or /get-in-touch.

AI Field Mapping

Every form field is semantically analysed and matched to your data — name, email, company, phone, message — with a confidence score per field.

CAPTCHA Solving*

reCAPTCHA and hCaptcha are detected and solved automatically via an integrated third-party solving service before the form is submitted.

Form Submission

The completed form is submitted exactly as a real user would — browser automation fills and submits each field accurately.

Result Tracking

Every site returns a success or failure result in real time. You see per-site status, error reasons, and credit spend in your live dashboard.

Credit Refunds

Failed submissions are never fully charged. System failures receive a 100% credit refund. External failures are charged at only 50%.

FAQ

Bulk Contact Form Submission — Common Questions

How many websites can I submit to at once?

Each campaign supports up to 30,000 website URLs. You can run multiple campaigns simultaneously, so there is effectively no ceiling on your total outreach volume.

What file formats are supported for bulk upload?

CSV, XLS, and XLSX files are all supported, up to 10 MB per upload. The platform uses AI heuristics to automatically identify which column contains website URLs — you don't need to rename columns or reformat your spreadsheet. Duplicate URLs are removed automatically before processing begins.

What happens if a site doesn't have a contact form?

If the platform cannot locate a contact form on a website, or the form fields cannot be mapped correctly, the submission is marked as failed and you receive a full credit refund for that site. You are never charged for a site that could not be submitted to successfully.

How does pricing work for bulk submissions?

Each successful form submission costs 2 credits. Optional AI personalization adds 1 credit per site. Credits are purchased in packs — the more you buy, the lower the per-credit cost, dropping as low as $0.025 per credit at the Enterprise tier. There is no subscription and credits never expire.

Can I pause a bulk campaign mid-run?

Yes. You can pause any live campaign at any point. Credits for unprocessed sites that have not yet been attempted are returned to your balance immediately. You can resume the campaign from exactly where it left off at any time.
