Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before launching your first campaign.

What is a contact form submission platform?

Software that automates website contact form inquiries at scale. Instead of manually visiting websites and filling in forms, Contact Form Blaster does it automatically across thousands of sites simultaneously using browser automation and AI.

Is this email marketing?

No. Contact form marketing delivers messages directly through company websites, bypassing email servers entirely. Your message is submitted through the target website's own contact form, so it never touches an email server or spam filter.

Do I need a subscription?

No. Contact Form Blaster operates on a pay-as-you-go credit system. Buy credits once and use them whenever — no recurring charges, no monthly fees, no contracts. Credits never expire.

Do I get charged for failed submissions?

You only pay for successful submissions. If a submission cannot be completed due to external factors beyond our system's control (such as issues with the target website), only 50% of the credits are charged. If the failure is due to our system, a full 100% credit refund is issued. You are never charged the full amount for a failed submission.

How does AI personalization work?

Enable the AI toggle when configuring your campaign. You write a single instruction (e.g., "Offer SEO services in a professional tone"). Our AI then generates a unique, quality-scored message for each website in your list — factoring in the target domain for context. AI personalization costs 1 extra credit per site and returns a 0–100 spam/quality score per message.

What file formats are supported for uploading?

CSV, XLS, and XLSX formats are all supported (up to 10 MB per file). The platform automatically detects which column contains website URLs using multi-pass AI heuristics — you don't need to rename or reformat your spreadsheet. Duplicate URLs are removed automatically.

Can I pause a campaign after it starts?

Yes. You can pause any live campaign at any point. You can resume the campaign from where it left off at any time.

How are CAPTCHAs handled?

Contact Form Blaster automatically detects reCAPTCHA and hCaptcha on contact forms and uses an integrated third-party CAPTCHA solving service to resolve them. If the CAPTCHA is solved, the submission proceeds normally. Sites where a CAPTCHA was encountered are charged the standard credit amount*

What are preset templates?

Presets let you save your entire campaign configuration — sender details, subject, message template, and AI instructions — so you can reuse it in future campaigns with one click. Perfect for agencies running similar campaigns for multiple clients or verticals.

What industries can I target?

Any industry with publicly accessible website contact forms. Upload any list of website URLs — whether it's e-commerce stores, law firms, real estate agencies, marketing companies, local businesses, or SaaS companies — and the platform handles the rest.

Is there a referral or affiliate program?

Yes. Every account receives a unique 6-character referral code. Share it — when someone signs up through your code, they receive bonus credits, and you earn a commission on their first purchase and every subsequent purchase. Commission earnings are tracked in your affiliate dashboard.

Do my credits expire?

No. Credits do not expire. Buy them now and use them months later — at any pace that suits your outreach calendar.

How do I log in — do you support Google?

Yes. You can register and sign in with Google OAuth for a one-click login — your email is auto-verified and 25 welcome credits are credited immediately. Standard email/password registration is also supported, with optional two-factor authentication (OTP via email).
